## 1. Business Diagnostic & Turnaround Strategy

**What It Is:**
Comprehensive operational analysis and strategic planning for businesses experiencing cash flow issues, inefficiency, or growth barriers. We examine every aspect of your business—financials, operations, inventory, vendor management, technology, and growth strategy—to identify root causes and design transformation roadmaps.

**Who It's For:**
- Companies with strong revenue but negative cash flow
- Businesses struggling with operational inefficiency
- Manufacturers with inventory or supply chain issues
- Service companies overwhelmed by manual processes
- Growing businesses hitting scaling barriers
- Companies preparing for market expansion

**How It Works:**
1.  **Initial Consultation (Free):** 60-minute discovery call to understand your business and challenges.
2.  **Deep Dive Diagnostic:** On-site or virtual comprehensive review of operations, financials, systems, and processes.
3.  **Analysis & Recommendations:** Detailed report identifying problems, quantifying impact, and proposing solutions.
4.  **Transformation Roadmap:** Prioritized action plan with timeline, resource requirements, and ROI projections.
5.  **Implementation Support:** We can execute the plan with you or hand off to your team.

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Freed working capital (typically 15-40% improvement)
- Reduced operational costs (average 20-30%)
- Improved cash flow visibility and forecasting
- Eliminated process bottlenecks
- Scalable operational foundation
- Clear growth roadmap with financial projections

**Investment:** Custom pricing based on business size and scope. Diagnostic phase starts at $5,000.
